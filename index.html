BTEC Business Unit 2 Part A checklist - 9 May 2022

Intro

During Part A...

  • You'll have access to the internet
  • You'll be given preparatory materials to analyse and write notes about
  • You'll have access to a word processor
  • You will be in relaxed exam conditions
  • Your notes will be checked, and cannot contain any pre-made promotional plans, and must be a maximum of 2 pages in length.

You will be provided with your annotated Part A document and these notes for your Part B exam.
